ZHili-byli ded da baba. Ded rabotal prodavcom v Makdake. I byla u nih Kurochka Zoya.

Snesla kurochka yaichko, da ne prostoe - zolotoe.

Ded bil - ne razbil.

Baba bila - ne razbila.

A myshka bezhala, hvostikom mahnula, yaichko upalo i razbilos'.

Plachet ded, plachet baba i govorit im Kurochka Zoya:

- Ne plach', ded, ne plach', baba: snesu vam novoe yaichko ne zolotoe, a prostoe!
